PassKiDukaan

We as team Neutrino try to create a solution to the problem statement given that is PassKiDukaan. An application that will provide a user-friendly interface that will seamlessly connect Seller with the Customer. Our application will work as a medium for the interaction of new customers with the trusted sellers. Fact is if a customer spends some time with the product selection and has a value conversation with the seller about the product before buying it. Then they feel reliable and value the product to buy it.This technique will be implemented by our application which will increase the profits of various physical stores.

Our application model will work on Online-to-offline (O2O) commerce in which potential customers from online channels (PassKiDukaan) make purchases in physical stores. Application main focus was on the high end products like: sports products, furniture, baby toys and electronic goods. As more chances of visit and conversion, compared to the FMCG market. According to a survey, nearly 74% of Indian consumers prefer purchase from retailers delivery at flexible timing compared to online shopping. So there is a huge market for physical stores. Our App will help the consumer to select from a bunch of trustworthy sellers around their area in the radius of 2-5 km. It will be easy and comfortable for the consumer to go and purchase. Customers want an in-hand experience of the products before buying them and our app will help to do it.
